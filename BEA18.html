BEA18 — Lurker (Scout/Observer-Class)

Threat Level: 1 – Minor Threat

Size: Slightly smaller than fighter

Height: ~1.2m upright

Classification: Beast

Location First Encountered: Ravencross Hills, collapsed mine shaft

Description

Lighter, more agile variant of the Lurker. Carries almost no offensive capability but uses high-frequency screeches and movements to distract intruders. Functions solely to bait attention, often feigning injury or vulnerability to lead victims into fighter’s ambush.

Behavior

  • Extremely mobile
  • Emits irregular clicks, screeches, or foot drags
  • Will retreat if directly threatened but never leaves the vicinity of the fighter
  • Occasionally circles prey to keep them disoriented or turn their back toward hidden threat

Traits

  • Plated but with thinner chitin
  • Bright internal fluid observed under extreme damage (possibly communication or marking fluid)
  • Not known to initiate combat alone

Weaknesses

  • Direct light appears to stun momentarily
  • Will not continue baiting if alone or cornered
  • Lacks claws, teeth incapable of breaching cloth armor

Utility

  • Fluids may have alchemical uses tied to signaling mixtures or trace markers
  • Thinner armor plates flexible; may serve as alchemically-reactive material base
  • Harmless when isolated; capture for study may be viable

Strategy

Use torchlight to bait both into movement. Do not pursue the scout blindly. Focus on fighter first, using auditory cues to track ambush approach. Isolate and eliminate the scout once the main threat is neutralized.
